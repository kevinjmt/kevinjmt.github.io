MY PERSONALITY

First passionate about video games, then mathematics, science, then technology, I am above all a curious enthusiast!

I am motivated by the values I defend on a daily basis. Seriousness and anticipation are part of it, especially in my work, but is also true in my personal life!

MY PASSION

THE STORY

If my passion started in video games, it only lasted a few years...

This passion has aroused in me a certain curiosity: How do video games work? How do computers/consoles/smartphones manage these games? What happens at the software level?

So many questions that should not be without answers...

Searching on the internet, on YouTube, I started to learn and practice with the little equipment I had.

While doing my internships at ON/OFF PC and Paragon ID, my interests have become a passion.

My passion has grown and become a part of my life!

MY BEGINNINGS

My beginnings were simple, handling a computer, knowing what is inside a computer, a phone or any other electronic device. In short, the hardware part. I have assembled, disassembled and repaired many PCs!

Then learn to master Android, Windows and manipulate the options, settings to optimize its hardware, customize it to the maximum. In short, learn to master the OS software, try different software tools. Thanks to these manipulations and my training courses, I am now able to debug many problems on Windows and Android!

During the confinement and my high school years, programming started to become a focus in my life without forgetting all the others!

Since all this time, I try to fit myself with all types of hardware, OS, software to have a versatility, a user-experience and a workflow as optimal as possible! Because anticipation and efficiency are the key to success and productivity!
