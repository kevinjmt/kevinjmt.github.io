DESCRIPTION

Paragon ID, formerly ASK, is a manufacturer of contact and contactless smart cards and RFID solutions. Founded in 1997, originally targeting the transportation ticket market, it has since diversified into the identity field with biometric passports.

When the company was acquired by the Paragon Group, ASK was renamed to Paragon ID. It is listed on the Paris Stock Exchange.
