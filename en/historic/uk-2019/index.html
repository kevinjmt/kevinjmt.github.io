DESCRIPTION

The purpose of this language trip was to discover the United Kingdom, which is directly related to the Baccalaureate 2021 (European mention).

During our trip, we visited London, Cambridge and exchanged with British people about the history of the United Kingdom, monuments, events. In addition, we stayed with a host family to learn more about the English language.

For me, this trip was a very good experience.
