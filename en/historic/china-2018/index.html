DESCRIPTION

This trip was offered to us by the Chinese government in order to discover the Chinese culture. Directly related to my living language 2 (Mandarin - Simplified Chinese).

We visited Beijing for 3 days and then visited and studied Chinese in Xi'An for a week and a half. We did activities to discover the Chinese culture, sports, customs, monuments.

For me, this trip was the best language experience of my life.
